The **Customer Feedback Summary Agent** answers questions about your customer
feedback. It retrieves entries from your data source, categorizes them by theme
and segment, and produces an executive summary with representative quotes and
concrete recommendations. Reach for it to turn a backlog of reviews, tickets, or
survey responses into something a team can act on.

<DocsBaseSwitcher base="eve" agent="feedback-summary" />

The **Eve** recipe runs in a single durable session: it **retrieves** feedback
with the paginated `get_feedback` tool, **categorizes** the entries by theme and
customer segment, then **summarizes** the top themes by volume and severity with
recommendations — grounded only in the feedback it actually fetched.

## Install

```bash
npx shadcn@latest add https://agentcn.vercel.app/r/eve/feedback-summary
```

## Prerequisites

- An [Eve](https://vercel.com/docs/eve) project (`npx eve@latest init my-agent`).
  The recipe installs files under `agent/`, which Eve discovers automatically.
- The `eve` package (installed automatically as a dependency of the recipe).
- A `FEEDBACK_API_URL` pointing at your feedback source (the `get_feedback` tool
  fetches paginated entries from it).
- For the **live preview below**, set `EVE_PREVIEW_URL` to a deployed Eve
  instance of this recipe. See the [Preview Backend](https://github.com/shadcn-labs/agentcn#preview-backend)
  notes in the README.

## Customization

- **Connect your source.** Point `get_feedback` at your database, support desk,
  or reviews API — keep the pagination + segment parameters.
- **Change the categories.** Edit `instructions.md` to use your own taxonomy
  (severity tiers, product areas, sentiment).
- **Swap the model.** Edit `agent/agent.ts` — a larger model for nuanced
  synthesis, a smaller one for speed.
- **Add a scorer.** Track summary quality across runs to tune the prompt.
